Poem On "Freedom Of Oppressors" By Emmanueal And David

Tied down for years;

We have faced pain, misery and pain for a long time.

The white colonized us;

But we were disqualified instead

of being qualified.

Lies on their tongue like chains;

To bound us for good.

Their laws that silenced the truth sunrise;

And made the top with the swat of the slaves.

Our lands became a reflection of our past.

We were tortured day and night;

But freedom was never away from our hearts;

No tyrant can hold our taste for freedom

No wall can bind the souls who aim for freedom.

Days and Nights are like hell

In the eyes of the oppressred.

Wake the lions in your veins,

Show them that we have a mind of our own;

We should rise and take what is rightfully ours;

Not turning back.

We shall rise with GOD, with a strength and might;

And also to shine brightlly and that light;

Will lit up the way to freedom.

Shaccterd voice will shout thunderousley;

And say "I Am My Own" no more chains.
